Chicken tots

Description

These chicken tots have a poutine vibe, and, while being much easier and faster to make than real poutine, satisfies in the same way. Saucy meat over potatoes is always a winner, but this creamy chicken sauce over tots will be your new favorite thing the minute you try it.

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil cooking spray
  • 1 lb. ground chicken
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. onion powder
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°. Grease a baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook chicken, breaking up meat with a wooden spoon. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until no longer pink, 7 minutes. Drain fat.
  3. Transfer chicken to a large bowl and stir in lemon zest and cheese.
  4. Form chicken mixture into tots and place on prepared baking sheet. Bake until golden and crispy, 20 minutes.
